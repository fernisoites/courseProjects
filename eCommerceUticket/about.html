Unlike other ticket selling company, we innovate a Machine Learning Algorithm which can calculate the reasonal price of a specific game based on history price and popularity. Integrated with our pricing algorithm, UTickets.com provides users with the most reasonable price indications. Since matches diverse from each other, our algorithm will recommend the most rationale price of tickets according to the popularity of each game. Which gives sellers hints of how to set prices for unique tickets, and help buyers to locate the rational region of ticket prices.
